Ticker Bundles: Readymade Stock Screener

Exploring Different Investment Strategies with Ticker Bundles

  1. Building Wealth with Growth Stocks: These bundles feature companies with strong earnings growth, innovative products, and a track record of outperforming the market.

  2. Finding Value in Value Investing Stocks: These bundles include undervalued stocks with strong fundamentals and the potential for price appreciation.

  3. High Dividend Stocks: A Steady Income Stream: Invest in bundles focused on high dividend stocks, providing a reliable source of income.

  4. Discover Hidden Gems: Explore bundles that include lesser-known companies with promising prospects.

How to Get Started with Ticker Bundles

  1. Visit Any Bundle: Explore the available Ticker Bundles (10 free and 10 paid) and choose one that catches your interest.

  2. Browse the Listed Stocks: Take a look at the list of stocks included in the bundle. This will give you an initial idea of the companies that make up the bundle.

  3. Customize if Desired: If you wish, you can customize the bundle by copying the formula in the screener and adding your own filter of ratios to it.

  4. Research Thoroughly: Once you have selected or customized a bundle, it's important to conduct further research on the stocks included. Dive deeper into their financials, performance history, and any relevant news or industry trends.
